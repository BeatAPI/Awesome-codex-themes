# Community code of conduct

We want a practical, curious, and welcoming project for people with different levels of systems, frontend, and design experience.

## Expected behavior

- Be specific, respectful, and patient in technical discussion.
- Critique code, evidence, compatibility, and design decisions—not people.
- Credit prior art, contributors, artists, and licenses accurately.
- Protect user privacy when sharing screenshots, logs, and reproduction material.
- Accept maintainers' responsibility to enforce the project's safety and intellectual-property boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing, publishing private credentials or conversations, deliberate license violations, and sustained disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close interactions, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Reports will be handled as privately as reasonably possible.

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and public spaces where someone is representing the project.
